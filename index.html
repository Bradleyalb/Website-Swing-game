Welcome to the Swing game! Play against whoever is randomly connected in order to become the best. If you are on the ground you must jump and then swing either left or right to continue your momentum. You can shoot either up or down to take down opponents. When you hit another player your score goes up but when you are hit your score goes down.

Controls:

  • Swing Left: A
  • Swing Right: D
  • Jump: E
  • Shoot Up: W
  • Shoot Down: S
